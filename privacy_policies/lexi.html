Lexi Drop — 隐私政策 / Privacy Policy

生效日期 Effective Date: 2025-09-30 最后更新 Last Updated: 2025-09-30
简要说明 Summary: 本页面为 Lexi Drop 应用的隐私政策。此应用当前不使用用户认证，即无强制登录或账户机制 (No authentication)。聊天内容仅用于生成 AI 回复，我们重视并保护你的数据安全。

1. 我们收集的信息 / Information We Collect

  • 聊天内容 Messages

    你发送给应用的文本会被用于生成 AI 回复。除非你主动选择“保存记录”，否则我们不会长期存储你的对话。 / Text you submit is sent to AI services to produce responses. We do not retain conversations long-term unless you explicitly choose to save them.

  • 匿名分析数据 Anonymous Analytics

    我们可能收集非个人化的崩溃日志与使用统计，用于改进应用稳定性与体验。此类数据不包含任何可识别你身份的信息。 / Non-personal crash logs and usage metrics may be collected to improve app stability and experience, which do not contain any personally identifiable information.

  • 不收集 No Account Data

    我们不要求电子邮件、姓名或其他身份凭证，应用无需登录即可使用。 / The app does not require email, name, or any login credentials to use.

2. 数据的使用方式 / How We Use Information

  • 生成 AI 回复并优化对话质量，确保你获得准确、有效的交互体验。 / To generate AI responses and improve conversation quality for an accurate and effective experience.
  • 修复应用错误、优化功能性能，提升整体使用流畅度。 / To fix bugs, optimize performance, and enhance overall app smoothness.
  • 我们不向第三方出售或交易用户个人数据，也不会将你的信息用于政策外的其他用途。 / We do not sell or trade user personal data to third parties, nor use your information for purposes beyond this policy.

3. 第三方服务 / Third-Party Services

本应用可能使用下列第三方服务，它们会按照各自的隐私政策处理数据。我们仅允许这些服务获取必要的信息以实现功能： / The app may use third-party services that process data according to their own privacy policies. We only allow these services to access necessary information to implement functions:

  • Google Generative AI (Gemini)

    用于生成 AI 回复。发送到该服务的文本仅用于即时生成回答，可能被该服务处理以改进其模型，具体请参见其隐私政策与使用条款。 / For generating AI replies. Submitted text is only used to generate instant answers and may be processed by the service to improve its models. See its privacy policy for details.

  • 可选分析 / 崩溃上报 Optional Analytics / Crash Reporting

    例如 Firebase Analytics 或 Crashlytics，当你未禁用时，它们会收集匿名事件或崩溃信息，帮助我们定位并解决问题。 / e.g. Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics, which collect anonymous event or crash information (if not disabled) to help us identify and fix issues.

4. 数据保存与删除 / Data Retention and Deletion

默认情况下，除非你在应用内主动保存对话或导出内容，我们不会长期保留你的聊天记录。所有未保存的对话会在会话结束后清除。 / By default, we do not persist chats long-term unless you actively save or export them in the app. All unsaved conversations will be cleared after the session ends.

如果你需要删除已保存的数据，请通过下方联系方式提出请求，我们会在 7 个工作日内响应并完成处理。 / If you need to delete saved data, please contact us using the details below, and we will respond and complete the process within 7 business days.

5. 未成年人政策 / Children's Policy

此应用不针对 13 岁以下儿童设计或推广。若发现未成年人数据被不适当地收集，请立即联系我们，我们将第一时间删除相关数据并采取预防措施。 / This app is not designed or promoted for children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child inappropriately, please contact us immediately, and we will delete the relevant data and take preventive measures promptly.

6. 安全与免责 / Security and Disclaimer

我们使用业界标准措施（如数据加密、访问权限控制）保护数据安全，但由于网络环境的复杂性，无法保证绝对安全。我们会持续更新安全策略，尽力降低风险。 / We use industry-standard measures (such as data encryption and access control) to protect data security, but due to the complexity of the network environment, absolute security cannot be guaranteed. We will continuously update security policies to minimize risks.
